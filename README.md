# Parkinson Speech AI Research

Welcome to the Parkinson Speech AI Research Organization.

We are a student-led research team focused on developing interpretable and reproducible Artificial Intelligence systems for speech-based neurological disease detection.

Our current research focuses on Parkinson's Disease detection using speech recordings by comparing traditional machine learning techniques with modern transformer-based speech models.

---

##  Research Objectives

- Develop robust speech-based Parkinson's Disease detection models.
- Compare handcrafted acoustic features with self-supervised speech representations.
- Build a reusable research framework for speech biomarker analysis.
- Promote reproducible AI research using modern MLOps practices.
